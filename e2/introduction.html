About the presenter

I am Revolution, some half bunnyhopper half coder who just enjoyed wiremod so much that he decided to make a whole website dedicated for teaching it. For long I wanted to get really good, but no expression 2 youtube tutorial series could really deliver to my expectations. I needed a tutorial series that could take me from zero to hero without the need for searching up the terminology for everything because it wasn't thoroughly explained, and without having to jump from series to series. That's why I want to change that, and give everyone the one knowledge base they need in order to excel in all wiremod-related things.
